# Demo Plan

## Demo Goal

Demonstrate how accounting operations can be automated and enhanced through AI and analytics.

---

## Demo Scenario

A company wants to monitor expenses, invoices, financial KPIs, and reporting from a centralized platform.

---

## Demo Flow

### Step 1

Import financial data.

### Step 2

Store transactions and invoices.

### Step 3

Run automation workflows.

### Step 4

Generate AI financial insights.

### Step 5

Display analytics dashboards.

---

## Demo Outcome

Show how financial operations become automated, visible, and data-driven.
