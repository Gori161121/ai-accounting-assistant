# Use Cases

## Use Case 1 — Invoice Tracking

A business needs to monitor issued invoices, payment status, and outstanding amounts.

The platform stores invoice data and prepares it for reporting.

---

## Use Case 2 — Expense Analysis

The system groups expenses by category and helps identify major cost areas.

---

## Use Case 3 — Monthly Financial Report

The platform combines invoice and expense data to calculate revenue, costs, and profit.

---

## Use Case 4 — Financial Intelligence Summary

AI-generated summaries help managers understand financial performance and identify areas that require attention.

---

## Use Case 5 — Reporting Automation

A scheduled workflow collects financial data and generates monthly financial reports automatically.
